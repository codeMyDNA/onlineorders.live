Get online orders from your customers

. . . while your community is in lockdown.

Easy to use.
Easy to install.
Easy to manage.

FREE. 



In this pandemic situation, many businesses are choosing to protect their staff
by closing their doors and only accepting phone orders.

Using this system, customers create an order online,
the store can call them to ask for a credit card, and then deliver or prepare the order for pickup.

Step 1

INSTALL HESK 3

You also need a web host.

If your hosting provider offers the Softaculous app installer, HESK 3 can be found there
(under ‘Customer Support’).

Otherwise go to hesk.com
to download the software. Follow their installation instructions.


Step 2

COPY ONLINEORDERS.LIVE FILES

Put the files into the ‘language’ folder of your HESK 3 installation.

Step 3

CUSTOMIZE

Log into the admin panel.
 
Under ‘Language’ select ‘English Orders’.

Then follow the customization instructions
in our Readme file.

Step 4

TEST
Make sure everything works, including sending email to customers.

. . .

And then TELL PEOPLE about it,
so they can
START ORDERING!

Go to GitHub to get started.

TAKE ME THERE

Helping communities survive a crisis. 

How do you keep life going while protecting essential services? 

Grocery stores are an essential part of every community.  But most of them are not tech-savvy.
I decided to help and set up a simple online order mechanism for the two stores in our island community of Islesboro, ME.  People were delighted.

Now I am sharing the ordering system with you, hoping that it will be useful.
